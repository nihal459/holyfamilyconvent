About Kashmir Valley School

Nestled in the picturesque valleys of Srinagar, Kashmir Valley School blends traditional Kashmiri culture with modern education to nurture young minds. Our institution stands as a beacon of learning amidst the serene Himalayan landscape.

2018 Best Regional Education Award

A Legacy of Excellence

Kashmir Valley School was established to preserve the rich cultural heritage of Kashmir while providing a world-class education. Our curriculum integrates local arts, languages like Kashmiri and Urdu, and environmental studies inspired by the valley's natural beauty.

We are committed to fostering resilience and creativity, preparing students to thrive in a globalized world while staying rooted in their Kashmiri identity.

Education in the Heart of Kashmir

Located in Srinagar, our school offers a unique learning experience with programs in science, arts, and vocational training. We emphasize sustainable practices, drawing from the region's ecological richness, and promote peace education to reflect Kashmir's historical ethos.

Our faculty, many of whom are local educators, bring a deep understanding of the community's needs and aspirations.

Governance

The school is overseen by a Board of Trustees, including local leaders and education experts, who meet biannually to guide our strategic direction. They ensure our policies align with Kashmir's cultural values and educational standards.

Transparency and community participation are central to our governance, fostering trust among parents, staff, and students.
