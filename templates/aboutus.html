About Holy Family Convent School

Located at Mirpur Ram Nagri Parole, Near Tehsildar Office, District Kathua, Jammu 184151, Holy Family Convent School blends Christian values with the cultural richness of Jammu to nurture young minds. Established by the Diocese of Jammu-Srinagar, our institution stands as a beacon of value-based education amidst scenic surroundings.

A Legacy of Excellence

Holy Family Convent School, established in 1989 by the Diocese of Jammu-Srinagar Education Society, is committed to imparting a humanizing education rooted in the life and teachings of Lord Jesus Christ. Our curriculum integrates moral values, local languages, and environmental awareness, inspired by the region's natural beauty.

We aim to foster resilience, discipline, and a sense of divine purpose, preparing students to thrive globally while staying connected to their cultural and spiritual roots.

Holistic Education in Jammu

Situated in Kathua, our school offers a holistic learning experience with programs in academics, sports, arts, and co-curricular activities. We emphasize sustainable practices and peace education, reflecting the region's cultural ethos and guided by Christian principles.

Our faculty, including local educators, bring a deep understanding of the community's needs and the school's mission to develop conscientious citizens.

Governance Structure

The school is overseen by a Board of Trustees, including members of the Diocese of Jammu-Srinagar Education Society and local education experts, who meet biannually to guide our strategic direction. They ensure our policies align with Christian values and the cultural ethos of Jammu.

Transparency and community participation are central to our governance, fostering trust among parents, staff, and students.
