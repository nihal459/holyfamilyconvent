Learning Approach

Our learning approach fosters holistic development, blending academic rigor with creative exploration. It is tailored for students from nursery to 10th class, offering:

  • Foundational Stage (Nursery–UKG): Play-based learning focusing on early literacy, numeracy, and social skills.
  • Primary (Classes 1–5): Core subjects with emphasis on critical thinking and foundational knowledge.
  • Secondary (Classes 6–10): Comprehensive studies including languages, sciences, and practical skills.

Co-curricular activities like arts and sports enhance the learning experience. Download Learning Approach Overview (PDF, 600 KB)

Academic Areas

Our academic areas are led by experienced educators, ensuring specialized instruction across disciplines. Key areas include:

  • Science: Hands-on experiments and practical learning.
  • Mathematics: Focus on problem-solving and logical reasoning.
  • Languages: English, Hindi, and additional language options.
  • Physical Education: Sports and wellness programs.

These areas collaborate to support interdisciplinary learning. View Academic Areas Details (PDF, 450 KB)

Class Schedules

Our class schedules are structured to optimize learning while allowing time for extracurricular activities. Key features:

  • Timings: 8:00 AM–2:00 PM (Nursery–UKG), 8:00 AM–3:00 PM (Classes 1–10).
  • Daily Structure: 6–8 periods of 40 minutes, including breaks.
  • Weekly Plans: Balanced mix of core subjects and activities.

Schedules are available on the parent portal or can be downloaded. Download Sample Schedule (PDF, 300 KB)
