Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to foster holistic development, blending academic rigor with creative exploration. Aligned with CBSE guidelines, it caters to students from LKG to Class 12, offering:

  • Foundational Stage (LKG–UKG): Play-based learning focusing on literacy, numeracy, and social skills.
  • Primary (Classes 1–5): Core subjects (English, Math, Science, Social Studies) with emphasis on critical thinking.
  • Secondary (Classes 6–10): Comprehensive subjects including Languages, Sciences, and Computer Applications.
  • Senior Secondary (Classes 11–12): Streams in Science (PCM/PCB), Commerce, and Humanities with elective options.

Co-curricular activities like arts, sports, and robotics enhance learning. Download Curriculum Overview (PDF, 600 KB)

Departments

Our academic departments are led by experienced educators, ensuring specialized instruction across disciplines. Key departments include:

  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology labs with hands-on experiments.
  • Mathematics: Focus on problem-solving and logical reasoning.
  • Humanities: History, Geography, Economics, and Psychology for Classes 11–12.
  • Languages: English, Hindi, and optional third languages (French, Sanskrit).
  • Commerce: Accountancy, Business Studies, and Entrepreneurship.
  • Physical Education: Sports and wellness programs.

Each department collaborates to deliver interdisciplinary projects. View Department Details (PDF, 450 KB)

Class Schedules

Our class schedules are structured to optimize learning while allowing time for extracurricular activities. Key features:

  • Timings: 8:00 AM–2:00 PM (LKG–UKG), 8:00 AM–3:00 PM (Classes 1–12).
  • Daily Structure: 6–8 periods of 40 minutes, including breaks.
  • Weekly Plans: Balanced mix of core subjects, electives, and activities.
  • Stream-Specific (Classes 11–12): Customized schedules for Science, Commerce, and Humanities.

Schedules are available on the parent portal or can be downloaded. Download Sample Schedule (PDF, 300 KB)
