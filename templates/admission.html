Admissions

We welcome students from LKG to Class 12 to embark on a journey of academic excellence and personal growth. Explore our enrollment process, requirements, and access downloadable forms below.

Your Path to Excellence

Enrollment Process

Our enrollment process is designed to be transparent and straightforward, ensuring a smooth transition for students and families. Follow these steps to join our school:

  1. Application Submission: Complete the online or offline application form available below.
  2. Document Verification: Submit required documents (listed under Requirements).
  3. Assessment/Interview: Students for Classes 1–12 may undergo an age-appropriate assessment or interview.
  4. Admission Confirmation: Receive confirmation and fee payment details within 7 working days.
  5. Orientation: Attend our welcome orientation for new students and parents.

Applications are accepted year-round, subject to seat availability. Contact our admissions office for assistance.

Requirements

To ensure a seamless admission process, please prepare the following documents and meet the eligibility criteria:

  • Age Criteria: LKG (3+ years), UKG (4+ years), Class 1 (5+ years) as of June 1, 2025.
  • Documents:
    • Birth certificate (copy)
    • Previous academic records (for Classes 1–12)
    • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
    • Passport-size photographs (2)
    • Proof of residence
  • Medical Form: Submit a health certificate signed by a registered medical practitioner.
  • Parent/Guardian ID: Copy of Aadhaar or other government-issued ID.

All documents must be submitted in English or with certified translations. Contact us for specific requirements for Classes 11–12 streams (Science, Commerce, Arts).
